# Olist-Business-Case-Study---Using-SQL

## Introduction to Business Case:
This is a Brazilian ecommerce public dataset of orders made at Olist Store. The dataset has information of 100k orders from 2016 to 2018 made at multiple marketplaces in Brazil. Its features allows viewing an order from multiple dimensions: from order status, price, payment and freight performance to customer location, product attributes and finally reviews written by customers.

[Dataset link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce)

The data is available in 8 CSV files:
- customers.csv
- sellers.csv
- order_items.csv
- geolocation.csv
- payments.csv
- reviews.csv
- orders.csv
- products.csv

By analyzing this extensive dataset, it becomes possible to gain valuable insights into Olist operations in Brazil. The information can shed light on various aspects of the business, such as order processing, pricing strategies, payment and shipping efficiency, customer demographics, product characteristics, and customer satisfaction levels.
